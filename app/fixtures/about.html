This is a week-end project (actually several week-ends) I was working, once the COVID-19 became a pandemic.

It was designd to keep you infomed on the progress of the contagion and also provide a way for you to keep track of the progress of the symptoms, even them being mild or absent (asymptomatic case).

The app focused on two main areas: symptoms progress tracking, and regional and country level statistics .

Symptoms progress tracking

  • Symptoms assessment can be done quickly, by pressing the Symptoms button. Answer by pressing green, yellow or red on individual questions.
  • If the app indentifies a change in symptoms, you will be asked to complete a the Roth Test, which is an instrument to evaluate the respiratory rate.
  • A Roth Test score over 25 indicates a normal respiation rate.
  • The complete history of the symptoms assessment results are accessible once the Results screen and can be inspected anytime.

Contagion Statistics

  • The app will use you location to retrieve the official statistics about the COVID-19 pandemic.
  • Reported cases and Death cases are provided at country level.
  • You can inspect the progress of the reported cases at local area level for the past 30 days in a animated graph.
  • You can change your current location, either local (in UK only) or country-wise from the On Map screen, where you can pick the place of interest.
  • UK - The data is retrieved from GOV.UK official site (https://coronavirus.data.gov.uk)
  • WorldWide - The data is retrieved from COVID-19 Data Repository by the Center for Systems Science and Engineering at Johns Hopkins University (https://github.com/CSSEGISandData/COVID-19).

Local Notifications

  • The app will schedule daily local notifications as reminders to assess your symptoms and eventually take the Roth Test.
  • the app will also send you prompts for symptoms assessments.
  • Your are encouraged to act and confirm the actions required by the reminders.
