Your privacy is very important and the app will not collect any data which is not absolutely necessary for its functionality.

The COVID-19 Tester is collecting the following data:

  • DeviceId - this is an identifier which is persistent accross successive usage of the app and will change once the mobile app is deleted. Since it's changing every time the app is deleted and reinstalled, it cannot be used to uniquely identify you.
  • Location - the location is used upon your consent to share it only to detrmine the country, region and area you currently are to show relevant statistics. Although the GPS coordinates are sent to the server, they are only used to determine the country, region and area and are not stored. Neverheless, the GPS coordinate, when provided, will be rounder to only two most semnificative digits to further protect your location.
  • IP Address - the IP Address is used to infer your broad location to the level of country, region and area in the absence of GPS coordinates. This is less accurate in detecting your current area, but it helps selecting the right country. We do not store the IP Addresses in our servers.
  • Hospital Code - this is a code which is randomly genrated at the first run of the app. Like the DeviceId, it does not persist if the application is deleted and reinstalled. Moreover, it will be wipd and regnerated if the Reset application data option from More Options is used. However, if provided by your doctor, it can be updated using the Change hospital code from More Options screen.
  • Symptoms Results - the symptoms results are collected and stored along with the Hospial code for grouping purposes and are intended only for statistical reasons. The symptom results are stored localy in the app and are not rretriebvable when the Hospital Code is changed.

Please contact us for any enquiry or concern regardind your privacy.
